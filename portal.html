Why KYC Matters
KYC unlocks your Pi mainnet wallet. Without it, your mined Pi stays locked. Complete the Chigalex1 training and gain access to all further Hub modules at no extra cost.
1
Open the Pi App
Required first step
Open your Pi Network app. Tap the menu (☰) and select "KYC". This opens the Pi Browser where verification happens. Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
💡If you don't see KYC yet, check the Pi Wallet app for Fast Track KYC — available to many new pioneers without waiting.
2
Use Fast Track KYC
Quickest route — recommended
Open the Pi Wallet app. Look for "Fast Track KYC". Tap it — this lets you verify and activate your mainnet wallet without waiting for 30 mining sessions.
✓Fast Track is AI-powered and usually gives results within hours. Recommended for all Chigalex1 Hub trainees.
3
Prepare Your Documents
Get these ready before starting
Have the following before tapping "Start KYC":
  • 📄National ID, Passport or Driver's License — valid, not expired
  • 📸Clear photos of your document — front and back
  • 🤷Selfie / liveness check — short live photo or video
  • 💡Good lighting — sit near a window, avoid shadows
  • 📶Strong internet (WiFi preferred) — avoid upload failures
4
Complete Verification
Follow each screen carefully
① Enter your real name exactly as on your ID.
② Upload your document — flat, no glare, all corners visible.
③ Take your selfie — look directly at camera, good light.
④ Submit and wait for processing.
⚠️Do NOT use a nickname. Name must exactly match your official document or application will be flagged as Tentative.
5
Understanding Your Status
What each result means
✓ Approved — Verified! Proceed to set up your Mainnet wallet.
⌛ Tentative — Extra verification needed. Complete liveness check, keep mining daily. This is NOT a rejection.
✗ Rejected — Usually a name mismatch or blurry document. Resubmit with clearer photos.
6
Activate Your Mainnet Wallet
After KYC is approved
① Open Pi Wallet app and complete the Mainnet Checklist.
② Write down your 24-word passphrase — store safely offline.
③ Your wallet address (starts with G...) is now active.
④ You can now pay for training in Pi!
🎓Wallet activated! Once you complete training, contact Alexander on +263 772 951 579 and all further Hub trainings unlock for free.
